AI-Powered Solutions and Technology

VIKI

Variational Intelligence for Key Insights

Advanced multi-stage AI platform for autonomous satellite telemetry anomaly detection that operates at two levels of intelligence. First, it answers is the satellite system in an anomalous state? — providing a system-level health alert with confidence probability. Second, if an anomaly is detected, it answers which specific channel or subsystem is responsible? — providing per-channel attribution that gives operators an immediate diagnostic starting point. VIKI does not replace operator judgement on the underlying physical cause, but it eliminates the manual effort of scanning dozens of channels to find where to look.

Performance Metrics (independently validated against ESA/ESOC Anomaly Detection Benchmark):

  • 99.8% System F1C — industry-standard composite metric (target ≥88%)
  • 99.6% System Precision — point-level (target ≥90%)
  • 100% System Recall — event-level detection (target ≥85%)
  • 88.6% F1C on 5-month sparse anomaly dataset (target ≥88%)

Key Capabilities:

  • System Health Monitoring with Real-Time Dashboards
  • Two-Level Intelligence: system-level health alert plus per-channel attribution
  • Diagnostic Starting Point: operators receive both the alert and the specific channel responsible
  • Adaptable to multi-database and multi-cloud scenarios
  • Adaptable to multi-satellite and multi-constellation scenarios
  • Data-Agnostic Platform with Minimal Customer Support Required

Applications: Satellite Operations, Ground Stations, Multi-Constellation Fleet Management

Status: TRL6 advancing to TRL8

Markets

ICQS develops and deploys AI-powered anomaly detection solutions across two primary market segments, addressing mission-critical operational requirements in both the commercial and defence domains.

Commercial

The commercial market represents a significant and growing opportunity for ICQS, spanning satellite operations, New Space constellations and Cyber-Physical Systems — three sectors where autonomous anomaly detection delivers immediate and measurable operational value.

Satellite operators and ground segment providers face an increasing challenge: as constellations grow in size and complexity, the volume of telemetry data generated far exceeds the capacity of traditional manual monitoring. VIKI addresses this directly — learning the normal behaviour of each satellite, detecting deviations in real time, and presenting operators with clear, actionable intelligence. The result is faster fault identification, reduced operator workload and improved mission continuity, without requiring any machine learning expertise from the operations team.

For New Space organisations and small satellite constellation operators, VIKI offers a deployment-ready AI platform that scales with the mission. With no proprietary hardware requirements and compatibility with any telemetry format, VIKI can be integrated into existing ground segment infrastructure rapidly and cost-effectively — making advanced anomaly detection accessible beyond the traditional large-programme market.

In the Cyber-Physical Systems domain, ICQS deploys AISAD — its adaptive AI platform for critical infrastructure protection. Industrial operators, energy sector companies and network infrastructure providers face persistent and evolving threats to their operational systems. AISAD provides continuous, intelligent monitoring that adapts its detection thresholds over time, identifying anomalous behaviour before it translates into operational disruption or safety incidents.
