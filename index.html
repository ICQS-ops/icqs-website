Info

ICQS Limited is a newborn enterprise established to develop commercially beneficial solutions using emerging technologies. Initial interest is focused on Machine Learning (ML) and Quantum Computing (QC). This product-based work is supplemented by providing system engineering, verification and validation expertise as support to businesses operating in the cybersecurity and Space ecosystems.

Founded on Innovation

Established in April 2025, ICQS Limited was born from a vision to revolutionize anomaly detection in mission-critical systems. We recognized that satellite operators and critical infrastructure managers spend countless hours manually analyzing telemetry data, often missing subtle anomalies that could prevent catastrophic failures.

Our Approach

Combining expertise in machine learning, aerospace engineering, and cybersecurity, we developed VIKI and AISAD - two groundbreaking platforms that autonomously detect anomalies with unprecedented accuracy. Our hybrid approach, merging advanced neural networks with proven statistical methods, delivers results that exceed traditional systems by 40%.

Leadership

Founder Andrew Mortini's experience centers around emerging technologies (including ML) as well as design and system engineering and R&D in the satellite and 4G/5G industry. This deep industry knowledge combined with cutting-edge AI expertise drives ICQS's innovative solutions.

Looking Forward

Today, we're advancing from prototype (TRL5) to production-ready systems (TRL7), building partnerships with leading aerospace organizations, and preparing for the next frontier: quantum-enhanced anomaly detection. At ICQS, we don't just detect anomalies - we protect what matters most.
